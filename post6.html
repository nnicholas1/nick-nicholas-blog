FHA loans help low- to moderate-income buyers with only 3.5% down required. Credit score of 580+ needed. Mortgage Insurance Premium (MIP) applies for life if down payment is under 10%.

VA Loans (0% Down)

Veterans and active-duty service members can get 100% financing with no PMI and competitive rates. Must qualify through the VA.

USDA Loans (0% Down)

USDA loans offer zero down for eligible rural/suburban properties. Income limits and location requirements apply.
